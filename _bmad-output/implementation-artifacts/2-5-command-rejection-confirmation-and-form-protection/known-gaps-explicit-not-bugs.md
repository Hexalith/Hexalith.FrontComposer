| G13 | **Post-reconnect idempotent Info-bar flood** (added 2026-04-17 post-elicitation Pre-mortem P0-A) ‚Äî under Story 5-4 durable replay, N in-flight optimistic commands replay as `Confirmed + IdempotencyResolved=true`, producing N Info bars. | G14 | **Client-side clock skew on abandonment timer** (added 2026-04-17 post-elicitation Chaos-2; mirror of Story 2-4 G14 `LastTransitionAt` skew) ‚Äî `FcFormAbandonmentGuard` computes `(TimeProvider.GetUtcNow() - _firstEditAt).TotalSeconds` on browser-side clock. | G16 | **Post-"Leave anyway" focus black-hole** (added 2026-04-17 per Sally review #4). Focus falls to `<body>` until the destination page's router renders and something takes focus. SR users hear "Leave anyway button activated" then silence until the new page announces. v0.1 ships without post-navigation focus management; **Story 3-x shell chrome owns post-nav focus transfer** (focus to H1 or `<main>` landmark of the destination page). 2-5 documents the cross-story dependency. | Focus-transfer on navigation is shell-level UX concern, not per-form. Epic 3 sidebar/breadcrumb + Story 3-x layout owns landmark rendering; 2-5 can't solve it without reaching into Shell chrome. | **Story 3-1** (Shell layout, theme & typography ‚Äî owns `<main>` landmark and post-nav focus contract) OR **Story 3-2** (sidebar navigation ‚Äî owns breadcrumb focus-return) |
