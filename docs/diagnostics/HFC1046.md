## Problem

Level 4 view override accessibility contract warning.

## Common Causes

`HFC1046` is allocated for Level 4 accessibility-contract warning metadata, but it is not a proven emitted diagnostic today. Active override accessibility enforcement is handled by the HFC1050-HFC1055 SourceTools analyzer, including Level 4 components discovered from `AddViewOverride` registrations.

## How To Fix

Use the active HFC1050-HFC1055 diagnostics to fix accessible names, keyboard reachability, focus visibility, `aria-live` parity, reduced-motion fallback, and forced-colors fallback in Level 4 replacements. Do not rely on `HFC1046` until a future catalog story either activates it or deprecates it.

## Example

```text
HFC1046: reserved Level 4 view override accessibility contract warning. Current active diagnostics: HFC1050-HFC1055.
```

## Suppression Guidance

Suppression is `allowed-with-rationale`. Prefer `.editorconfig` or a narrowly scoped pragma for analyzer diagnostics.

## Migration/Deprecation

No migration action is required unless a future diagnostic-catalog story changes this diagnostic lifecycle.
